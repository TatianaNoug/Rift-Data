Home - Application description

This app is the first demo version of our League of legends match analysis tool.

Charts Page

You will find various charts. They will permit you to create your own strategy based on what works the most

Roles Page

You will be able to choose a role (top, jungle, mid, adc, support) and after a charging time, 3 champions with the best winrate and 3 champions with the worst winrate will be returned.

Be careful, for now the app doesn't allow you to load a second role right after the first one. Visit a new page first and come back later
